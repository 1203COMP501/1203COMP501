Adolescent technology addition

Describe your chosen technology/topic:

Adolescents who spent more time on technology or social media, and less time on person to person interaction and exercise had lower psychological wellbeing

Why is it significant and/or has important implications for society:

Adolescents who spent majority of their time on devices are more likely to report mental health issues, this results in a generation of adolescents developing mental problems, which could potentially cripple society.
